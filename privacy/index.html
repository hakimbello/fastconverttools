Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

FastConvertTools.com ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website fastconverttools.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our file conversion service:

  • Files you upload: Files uploaded for conversion are processed on our servers and automatically deleted within 1 hour of conversion.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, browser type, and device type through Google Analytics.
  • No account data: We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or passwords.

2. How We Use Your Files

Files you upload are used solely for the purpose of conversion. We do not:

  • Read, analyze, or store the content of your files beyond what is necessary for conversion
  • Share your files with any third party
  • Use your files for training, advertising, or any other purpose

All uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing. Browser-based tools (JPG to PNG, Resize Image) process files entirely on your device — no files are uploaded to our servers at all.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following third-party services that may use cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements and may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use CloudConvert to process certain file conversions (MP4 to MP3, PDF to Word, Compress PDF). Files sent to CloudConvert are subject to their privacy policy, available at cloudconvert.com. CloudConvert automatically deletes files after processing.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your files during upload and conversion. All file transfers use HTTPS encryption. Files are automatically deleted within 1 hour and are never shared with unauthorized parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@fastconverttools.com
