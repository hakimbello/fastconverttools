Frequently Asked Questions

Does my file get uploaded to your servers?

+

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your image never leaves your device. It is 100% private.

Is there a file size limit?

+

No file size limit since everything runs in your browser. Very large images (50MB+) may take a few seconds.

Will I lose image quality?

+

PNG is a lossless format. Your converted image will maintain the full quality of the original JPG.

Why convert JPG to PNG?

+

PNG supports transparency, is lossless, and is better for logos, screenshots, and graphics. JPG is better for photos.
